AnyPortal:
Zero-Shot Consistent Video Background Replacement

Overview

We introduce AnyPortal, a novel zero-shot framework for video background replacement that leverages pre-trained diffusion models. Our framework collaboratively integrates the temporal prior of video diffusion models with the relighting capabilities of image diffusion models in a zero-shot setting. To address the critical challenge of foreground consistency, we propose a Refinement Projection Algorithm, which enables pixel-level detail manipulation to ensure precise foreground preservation. AnyPortal is training-free and overcomes the challenges of achieving foreground consistency and temporally coherent relighting.
